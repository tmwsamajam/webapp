Privacy Policy

How we handle information shared through this website.

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Tambaram Malayalee Welfare Samajam (“TMWS”, “we”, “our”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use our website and how it is used.

Information we collect

This is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not operate a backend database. If you contact us via email or WhatsApp links, the information you choose to share is received directly by TMWS through those channels.

Cookies & local storage

We may store a theme preference (light/dark mode) in your browser’s local storage to improve your experience. No advertising cookies are used by this site.

Third-party services

Pages may load fonts from Google Fonts and embed Google Maps. Those services may process technical data according to their own policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, email info@tmwsamajam.org.
