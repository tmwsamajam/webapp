About TMWS

Our story is one of belonging — rooted in Kerala’s heritage and grown in the heart of Tambaram.

History

A Samajam built on unity

Tambaram Malayalee Welfare Samajam was founded by families who wanted a warm, organized space to celebrate culture and support one another away from home.

Over the years, TMWS has grown into a trusted community platform — hosting festivals, educational initiatives, health camps, and welfare programs that touch hundreds of households.

Purpose

Mission, vision & objectives

Mission

Strengthen Malayalee families through culture, welfare services, and meaningful connection.

Vision

An inclusive Samajam where tradition thrives alongside modern community care.

Objectives

Promote heritage, enable education, advance health access, and respond compassionately in need.
