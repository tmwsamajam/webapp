About TMWS

Mission, vision & purpose

We nurture belonging for Malayalees in Tambaram — preserving heritage while building practical support for every generation.

Mission

To strengthen Malayalee families through cultural programs, welfare services, and meaningful community connection.

Vision

A thriving, inclusive Samajam where tradition and modern life coexist — and every member feels supported.

Objectives

Promote culture, enable education, organize health camps, and respond with compassion in times of need.
