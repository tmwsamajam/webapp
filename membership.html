Eligibility

Who can join

Membership is open to Malayalee individuals and families residing in Tambaram and neighbouring areas who share TMWS values of unity, culture, and welfare.

Steps

How to join

Step 1

Download the form

Get the membership form from the Downloads page or request it via Contact.

Step 2

Submit details

Complete the form and share it with the Secretary along with the membership fee.

Step 3

Welcome aboard

Once approved, you’ll receive confirmation and event updates from TMWS.
